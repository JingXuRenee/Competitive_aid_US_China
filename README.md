# Competitive_aid_US_China
This repository contains the complete, working R script, data and output figures for the Final Project of my course: POLI_3148 Data science in politics and public administration in 2023 Fall.

The topic of the project is The Competitive Aid: A Case Study of US and China Aid to African Countries.

keywords: Foreign Aid, China Aid, US Aid, African Aid.

# Data
Aid data of China is from AIDDATA `https://china.aiddata.org/`

Aid data of the US is from OECD Statistics/Development/Flows by Provider and Recipient `http://stats.oecd.org/`:
  
    Inflow: total official flows by country and region (ODA+OOF)/gross
  
    ODA: Aid (ODA) disbursements to countries and regions/Memo: gross
  
    Grants: Aid (ODA) disbursements to countries and regions/Grants, Total
  
    Data by Sector: Flows based on individual projects (CRS)

Control variable data:
  
  Recipient needs: 
    
    Infant_mortality, GDP per capita, Population. World Bank open data, `https://data.worldbank.org`
  
  Recipient merits:
    
    Control of corruption, Political stability, Government effectiveness, Regulation quality. World Bank governance indicators, `https://www.worldbank.org/en/publication/worldwide-governance-indicators/documentation#4`
    
    Freedom Index. Freedom house, `https://freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-world`
